It&#039;s finally here. The mac app store.

Thu 06 January 2011
By kristian

In Apple, Mac.

Let me start by saying, I really love my mac - and all the stuff that comes with it. When I, two years ago, switched from Windows Vista to Mac OS X I was thrilled by the general quality of the applications available for OS X. Everything looks nicer and works better. This is why I have been looking forward to the release of the Mac App Store. A quick view shows, what Apple showed on their prelaunch site months ago: `|Mac App Store Overview|`_ It looks a bit like iTunes, but its entirely separated (thank god!). I initially tried to download MindNode for Mac.  Its was surprisingly easy and the software was automatically added to the dock. Currently the selection is a bit narrow and many of the apps available are iphone ports and to be honest, I dont think Angry Birds is as fun on the Mac as it is on my iPhone. However, the Danish App Store reviews gives another impression. If you didn't update your Mac yet, you can do it from System Preferences. The download is not too big and after a fast restart you are ready to try the brand new Mac App Store.
